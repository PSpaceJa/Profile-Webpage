Personality

While personality tests can give great insight, I find them sometimes a bit triggering. I remember doing one in January of this year and I was excited by the results and thought to myself this is so amazing because it seemed to describe me perfectly.

Fast forward to now when we are almost at the end of the year and it was such a challenge answering these questions because where I am now is not where I was in January. It was hard answering some of these questions because they were so absolute and I feel like I was neither of the choices presented but more in the middle. I guess its because we are always changing.
